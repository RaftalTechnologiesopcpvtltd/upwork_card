Manage FAQs

To create a new blog post, log in to your admin panel and navigate to the Blogs section. Click on "Add New Blog" button, fill in all the required information including title, content, and category, then click on "Save Blog" to publish it.

To manage subscription plans, navigate to the Subscription Plans section in your admin panel. Here you can view all existing plans, edit them, or add new ones by clicking on "Add New Plan" button.

To update the homepage slider, go to the Sliders section in your admin panel. You can add new slides, edit existing ones, or change their order. Make sure the "Active" toggle is on for slides you want to display.

To respond to contact form submissions, navigate to the Contact Submissions section in your admin panel. Click on a message to view its details, and use the provided email information to respond to the inquiry.
